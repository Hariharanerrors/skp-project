OBJECTIVE

The objective of the State Conference was to bring young budding engineers together with Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and English of matching interests on a common platform to share new ideas, experiences, research and knowledge obtained in various domains of science and technology. The State Conference aims to foster and build a strong network among engineers, discuss information in relevant domains of science with the motive of seeding future developments and give an opportunity to its participants to exchange latest research results, ideas and applications in the Recent Trends in science and technology.
